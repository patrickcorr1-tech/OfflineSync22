## Client Onboarding Checklist

### Before Project Start

**Week -1 (Proposal Accepted):**
- [ ] Send service agreement for signature
- [ ] Send deposit invoice
- [ ] Create project folder (Google Drive/Dropbox)
- [ ] Send welcome email with:
  - Project timeline
  - Key contact information
  - What to expect next
  - Any prep work required

**Week 0 (Kick-off):**
- [ ] Receive signed agreement
- [ ] Receive deposit payment
- [ ] Schedule kick-off meeting
- [ ] Send meeting agenda

---

### Kick-off Meeting Agenda

**Attendees:** Client project lead, IT contact (if applicable), Patrick  
**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Format:** Video call

1. **Introductions (5 min)**
2. **Project Recap (10 min)**
   - Confirm scope and objectives
   - Review timeline
   - Identify any changes since proposal
3. **Access Requirements (15 min)**
   - What systems need access
   - Who provides credentials
   - Security protocols (2FA, VPN, etc.)
4. **Communication Plan (10 min)**
   - Weekly status calls? Email updates?
   - Emergency contact process
   - Preferred communication channels
5. **Risk and Constraints (10 min)**
   - Blackout dates (client holidays, critical business periods)
   - Known technical debt or issues
   - Compliance requirements
6. **Next Steps (10 min)**
   - What I need from client this week
   - What I'll deliver this week
   - Schedule first technical session

**Action:** Send meeting notes within 24 hours with action items assigned.
