## POSTING SCHEDULE & BEST PRACTICES

### Optimal Posting Times (UK):
- **Tuesday-Thursday:** 8:00-9:00 AM or 12:00-1:00 PM
- **Monday/Friday:** 8:00-9:00 AM only
- **Avoid:** After 5 PM or weekends (lower engagement)

### Content Mix:
- 40% Educational (tips, how-to, explanations)
- 30% Story/Case Study (results, behind-the-scenes)
- 20% Engagement (questions, polls, discussion)
- 10% Direct CTA (offers, booking links)

### Engagement Strategy:
1. **Post** at optimal time
2. **Wait 30 minutes**, then respond to all comments
3. **Engage with 5-10 other posts** in your network
4. **Share to relevant groups** (if appropriate)
5. **Add value in comments** on popular posts

### Hashtag Strategy:
- Use 3-5 hashtags per post
- Mix of:
  - Broad: #Construction #Technology #Business
  - Niche: #ConstructionIT #SiteManagement #Microsoft365
  - Personal brand: #[YourName] #[YourCompany]
