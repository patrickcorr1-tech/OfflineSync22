## Their Goals (5-7 minutes)

"Let's talk about the ideal outcome. If we work together and this project is successful, what does that look like in 6 months?"

Listen for:
- Measurable outcomes ("reduce IT tickets by 50%")
- Business objectives ("enable remote work", "win that contract")
- User experience improvements ("people stop complaining about email")

**Follow-up:**
"Of those goals, which is the most important? What would you compromise on if needed?"
