## PHASE 3: Link Building Tactics (Month 2-3)

### 5. Broken Link Building

**Process:**
1. Find relevant blogs in your niche
2. Use Chrome extension "Check My Links" to find broken links
3. Email the site owner with a helpful replacement

**Template:**
```
Subject: Broken link on your [Topic] page

Hi [Name],

I was reading your excellent article on [Topic] and noticed a broken link 
to [resource that no longer exists].

I recently published a comprehensive guide on [similar topic] that might 
be a good replacement: [your URL]

It covers:
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2] 
- [Point 3]

Hope this helps your readers!

Best,
Patrick
```

Patrick Corr

### 9. Skyscraper Technique

**Step 1:** Find popular content in your niche
- Search: "Microsoft 365 migration guide"
- Look for content with lots of backlinks (use Ahrefs free trial or Ubersuggest)

**Step 2:** Create something better
- Make it more comprehensive
- Update outdated information
- Add visuals/checklists
- Make it easier to read

**Step 3:** Reach out to sites linking to original
```
Subject: Better resource on [Topic]

Hi [Name],

I noticed you linked to [Original Article] on your [Page Name]. 
It's a great resource, but I noticed some of the information is outdated 
(particularly [specific point]).

I just published an updated guide that covers:
- [New point 1]
- [New point 2]
- [Something the original missed]

Here's the link: [Your URL]

Thought your readers might find it useful as an additional resource.

Best,
Patrick
```
